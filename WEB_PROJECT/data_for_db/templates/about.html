About Di_Just

Sometimes we all want to check saved videos, links or posts.
But soon we discover that our notes are in a complete mess.
Thus, we lose motivation to search for the information we need.
As a result, we continue procrastinating.

Di-Just was made to help people sort information into digests.
So, you will have all information you need in a strict order.
You can also share your digests with other people (or don't share, it's up to you =))
